## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Save frequently** - HMR auto-reloads browser
2. **Check DevTools** - F12 for styling and errors
3. **Mobile first** - Always test on small screens first
4. **Comments help** - All components have JSDoc
5. **Reuse components** - Button, Card are very flexible
